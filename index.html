Custom 6 months 5/3/1 PPL Program Generator

Easy-to-print format. Steady linear progress with deloads. Different assistance work every time to diversify sessions.

Simple and effective :

1. Enter your five Maxes in the input fields below.
2. Press the button to generate your custom program.
3. Print your new program directly from your browser.

This 6 months program is ideal for people who recently started the gym or are coming back to the game and are lost as to what to do. To get started, you'll need the One Rep Max for the five main compound exercices that are the core of this program.

For each of the main lifts, work up to a weight that you are only able to complete 3-5 reps of with good bar speed. Then, Plug the weight in and reps into this One Rep Max calculator. Then, put the resulting One Rep Max for each of the main lifts in the input fields below and your program will be automatically generated.

Notes :

  • Rest time between sets should be betwenn 1min - 2min, depending on how much you need.
  • Your workout should be intensive and be max one hour. There's no point to workout longer.
  • If you feel like it, feel free to do the assistance in supersets.
  • The program is in pounds (lb).
  • Legend : OHP = Overhead Press; BB = Barbell; DB = Dumbell.
