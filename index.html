Custom 6 months 5/3/1 PPL Program Generator

Easy-to-print format directly from browser. Steady linear progress with deloads. Different assistance exercices to diversify sessions.


To get started, you'll need the One Rep Max for the five main compound exercices Ã  that are the core of this program. How do you get that, you ask?

For each of the main lifts, work up to a weight that you are only able to complete 3-5 reps of with good bar speed. Then, plug the weight and reps into this One Rep Max calculator. This will give you your 1RM. Put the resulting 1RM for each of the main 5 lifts in the input fields below and your program will be automatically generated.

Notes :

  • Feel free to do the assistance in supersets.
  • The program is in pounds (lb).
  • Legend : OHP = Overhead Press; BB = Barbell; DB = Dumbell.
